---

## Challenges

When we started in the summer of 2025, my partner and I faced several design challenges. We initially set a strict constraint of achieving both the extension mechanism and rotor tilt mechanism using only two servos.  

Because of this constraint, we spent many work sessions struggling to develop solutions we were satisfied with. When I revisited the project, I removed that constraint, which significantly simplified the design and improved feasibility.
